Ink Wash

3320 x 3320

10" x 10"

2024

A birthday gift for my friend MTD. This is his Fortnite main, Keleritas.

I used Fortnite Porting to get the game model into Blender, and gave him a shader that blocked out different darkness levels. I posed the model how I wanted, and used the render as reference for this piece. I actually printed it out to look at as I worked on the real one. I'm happy with how it came out, and even think the one I made looks better than the orginal render. I made some changes as I made it that felt appropriate.
