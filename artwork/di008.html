Marksman

Digital

2560 x 2560

2025

I started drawing this back in February 2024, when I was starting to talk alot more with my friend Rahim.

Marksman is to be the main character of a videogame, and they have had other designs in the past. This was my attempt at doing my own version, with feedback from Rahim.

The main inspiration for the character is this video of Ski Man. It features this suit that allows the wearer to ski in several positions, derivitave of buggy rolling. The influence should be pretty apparent when looking at the design. This time around, I have quite a few different versions of the lineart saved.

With this drawing, I was going for a very specific look, so the rendering might seem a bit different to other similar drawings of mine. I really wanted it to feel like something out of digimon, or something adhearing to the gadgetpunk style.

I'm still interested in itterating on this design, so you may see more drawings of Marksman that look different.
