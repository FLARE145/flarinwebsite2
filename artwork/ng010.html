Spore Creature

Digital

2435 x 2256

2022

I made this for a video thumbnail on my youtube channel. I played Spore on stream and created this creature called Lil Square. The two creatures on its left are Pescy and Smashburger, which are species you can encounter in the game by default. This didn't take much time to paint, but I spent a good while tweaking the contrast and saturation after I was done. Even the cropped down version for youtube has a slightly different look.

Here's what the thumbnail looks like. Also you can click on it to go the video itself.

A this is what it looked like before all of the technical tweaks. As you can see, I tend to paint with low contrast and desaturated colors.

But yeah, very fun. I think I might mess with the crop again though. Seems a little off balance, although I suppose thats because I made it with purely the thumbnail in mind. It wasn't really made to be a standalone piece. Still cool though.
