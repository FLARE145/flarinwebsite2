Plantita

Digital

1920 x 1440

2020

This was experimental for me at the time. I had become obsessed with placing 2d drawn characters in 3d environments, and I was determined to make it look good. Unfortunately, I had to rush to finish this for school. The background suffered a lot from this, but its not the worst thing in the world. This piece still serves as a thematic starting point for everything I made between late 2020 and early 2021.
