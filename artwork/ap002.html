Hopefull Hills

Digital

1920 x 1080

2020

One of my favorite works that utilizes digital 3d to compliment a 2d drawing. This scene is not as negative as you might expect from this common setting. I wanted to make a hopeful post-apocalyptic scene, and I did this by embracing some almost pastel-like colors.

This work-in-progress picture shows how the envoirnment was layed out. I did more 2d work on top of the final render to bring everything together more seamlessly.
