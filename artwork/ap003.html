Alleyway Hangout

Digital

2400 x 3000

2020

A somewhat comforting warm city night scene. I learned some more techniques to hide the use of 3d graphics in this one. Bold hand drawn oulines make a big difference. The image was also put through some weird paint filter software that gave it this texture. I don't think I will be using that as much for future peices.

This work-in-progress picture shows the environment before I drew extra detail over it. The characters were rendered as objects in the environment, but other details like outlines had to be drawn over the render. I also found this doodle which seems to be the original inception of my idea for this artwork.
