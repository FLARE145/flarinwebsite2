Dark Winds

Dark Winds is a tv series that my sister recommended I watch, and I can confirm that it was a good recommendation. It takes place in the 70's on the Navajo Nation and plays out as a mystery-solving thriller. The first season is probably one of the most satisfying seasons of tv I've ever seen. The second season is not as tight, but it was still fun to see more of it. If you're willing to commit some time, I'd recommend watching the first two episodes. I think you'd be able to judge if the show is for you or not based on that.
