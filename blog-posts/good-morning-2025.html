Boxkid Progress

Yeah it's been pretty slow. I've been busy with work/ Christmas preparation/ Christmas recovery/ New years preparation/ New years recovery/ time with friends. Still, I have gotten 10 pages of the prologue done, which I am quite happy with. My goal is to release Boxkid this month, so I will have to focus a lot more on it in the coming days.

I recently did a read through of Chapter 1 now that it's been a while, and I can definitely say it came out much better than expected. It might not seem like it because of how I talk about Boxkid now, but this was supposed to be my rough project. The one where I don't care if things go wrong or if the story goes down the wrong route. Everything about it was designed around the idea of, "draw forward and anything goes." In writing the first chapter, things got way more defined. Now it's actually pretty cool. There's a balance in my head of how much is an attempt at serious storytelling and how much is random ideas I like. And I like the balance it has right now. I'm very happy with Chapter 1. Very curious to see what others would think too.

Stay tuned. This month maybe. Hopefully.
