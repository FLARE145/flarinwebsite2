I went on a podcast

10/9/22

This actually happened a good while ago now. In June, I went on the Off the Wall podcast with ThatJohnnyGuy. At the time, I really wanted to get into interacting with more people online, so it was a well-timed opportunity.

I've actually been too afraid to listen back to the whole thing, so I don't know how well it actually turned out. I assume that it comes across as pretty rambley because of the raw sort of style the show has. I had intended to talk about some strange funny stories of mine, but we ended up going through my animation work and stuff. I feel like I probably sound either full of myself, or really boring. I'm not sure anything I said was valuable or interesting. In any case, it was still fun and I'm grateful for the experience.

Unfortunately, I did this like right before some life-shaking stuff happened, so I didn't end up talking about the podcast here on my blog. Or anywhere else I think? Anyways yeah, doing a lot better now so that's nice. I'd really love to get back to interacting with people again like I had intended to. There's still a lot I have to do before I can get to that though.

Hope you enjoy the podcast episode if you choose to listen to it. Idk what my next blog post will be because I kind of can't work on a lot right now. See you when I remember something interesting to talk about
