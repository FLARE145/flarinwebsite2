My first furry art

1/18/24

If you don't follow me too closely, which is unlikely considering you are reading my blog, this title must appear rather frightening. However, I can assure you that there is no reason to feel alarmed.

I recently went live drawing my first furry fanart. It was of Kimiko Five-Tails. I was surprised when I realized I've never drawn a furry, so I wanted to get my first one right. I think I succeeded and now the doors are open for me to make money. I probably won't though. Advertising commissions is hard I think.

In other news, I added another batch of artwork to the art page. You can check it out by going to the series tab. I've added all of the endstills from Fun Friday. Endstill is the word I use to refer to the funky image at the end of each FF episode. They are pretty cool so I thought they should go up on the site. Plus, I'm having a hard time figuring out how to categorize more of my work, and this was an easy category to add. Oh yeah and this does mean that I have migrated the full artwork images off of github. They are all hosted on google now so I have to worry less about storage.

Thanks for reading. I just felt the need to somewhat document this particular event. I don't like how I end most of these posts, so I think I'm just going to charge up the word bruahsani. I'm going to end this post by saying that, but know that in this context, I want bruahsani to symbolize an entire goodbye. It's hard to explain what I mean. I guess kind of like an amen. Anyways, bruahsani
