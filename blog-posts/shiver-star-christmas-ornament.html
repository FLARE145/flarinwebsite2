12/16/25

Similar to the pumpkin I decorated for Halloween at work, I've decorated an ornament for Christmas. I had multiple ideas for how to decorate it, but I completely ran out of time! I ended up with just 1 day to work on it, but thankfully, I came up with a new idea that didn't require much effort.

The idea is "Earth frozen over." I was inspired by the Kirby 64 level Shiver Star. The level is well known by Kirby fans because of the implication that this is what happened to Earth in the Kirby cannon. I have no idea how well that holds now that the forgotten land is a thing. I'm not a real Kirby fan, I've just absorbed Kirby-ness through people around me, so I really like Kirby.
